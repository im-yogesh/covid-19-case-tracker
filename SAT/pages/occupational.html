Contact the occupational health
provider at your workplace.

  • Tell your occupational health provider (or supervisor) that you have been in
    contact with someone diagnosed with COVID‑19. You may be asked to wear a
    mask over your nose and mouth to protect yourself and those around you.
    • Check your temperature twice a day for 14 days.
    • Watch for COVID‑19 symptoms. Learn more about COVID‑19 and how you
      can protect yourself and others on the CDC website.
  • You (they) may be eligible for COVID‑19 testing. Visit your health department’s website or talk to your healthcare provider
    for more information. To find a testing location near you, visit the HHS website.
